Welcome to The Old Richmond Light Show's website. We are happy that you have arrived!

For many years this Old Richmond light display has grown larger and larger. It has taken a lot of time and effort to get to this point. Growing from a few hundred to nearly 30,000 lights. The show is animated to music and controlled by over 88,576 channels of Falcon, Xlights and Lynx Express.

This spectacular show is a sight to see!

Sunday thru Saturday 5:30pm til 11:00pm The lights are turned on by December 1st and turned off January 1st
